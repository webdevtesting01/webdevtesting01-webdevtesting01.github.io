Denver Data Storytellers
We are a community of individuals passionate about telling stories effectively with data.

Events

Our free events are facilitated by community members and center around our values of practicality,
diversity of perspectives, sharing resources, and cultural sensitivity.

Data Presentations

From Lunch and Learns to our annual 'Behind the Design' series, we regularly hold events to inform and inspire folks on all things data storytelling.

Book Club

The DDS book club brings together data lovers to explore the practice of data storytelling through the lens of authors in the field. We typically hold a book club quarterly- check out the newsletter for what we're reading next!

Data Happy Hours

Throughout the year, we host informal happy hours to bring together the denver data storytelling community.
